SuperLotto Plus® 2nd Chance Multiplier Official Rules

Purpose
 The California Lottery (“Lottery”) is conducting a SuperLotto Plus 2nd Chance Multiplier Promotion as an additional opportunity for 2nd Chance players to receive more entries into the weekly SuperLotto Plus 2nd Chance pool draws.

Promotion Period
The SuperLotto Plus 2nd Chance Multiplier Promotion will begin on May 31, 2015 and end on July 11, 2015 at 11:59pm Pacific Time. The Lottery reserves the right to terminate or extend this promotion at any time.

Prize
Five weekly winners of $15,000 for each SuperLotto Plus 2nd Chance drawing.

How to Enter
During the promotional period, for certain SuperLotto Plus 2nd Chance webcodes submitted, a player will receive a multiplier that will either give the player 2x or 3x the standard entries into the weekly SuperLotto Plus 2nd Chance pool draws. See details below about the multiplier:
• For every $1.00 – $4.00 SuperLotto Plus purchased per ticket, a player will continue to receive the standard entries into the 2nd Chance system
• For every $5.00 – $9.00 SuperLotto Plus purchased per ticket, a player will receive 2x the standard entries into the 2nd Chance system
• For every $10.00 SuperLotto Plus purchased per ticket, a player will receive 3x the standard entries into the 2nd Chance system

Advance Plays®:
• For every $1.00 – $4.00 SuperLotto Plus Advance Play purchased per ticket, a player will continue to receive the standard entries into the 2nd Chance system
• For every $5.00 – $9.00 SuperLotto Plus Advance Play purchased per ticket, a player will receive 2x the standard entries into the 2nd Chance system
• For every $10.00 + SuperLotto Plus Advance Play purchased per ticket, a player will receive 3x the standard entries into the 2nd Chance system

Players must login to their existing 2nd Chance account or create an account at CA Lottery in order to submit a SuperLotto Plus 2nd Chance webcode. Drawing participants may not enter more than 500 submissions for all 2nd Chance drawings per calendar month based on the Lottery’s Submission Limitation Policy. Learn more about the Submission Limitation Policy.

Drawings
 SuperLotto Plus 2nd Chance Drawings will take place weekly in accordance with Lottery Regulations and procedures. Five alternate winners will be chosen per drawing in the event a selected entry is ineligible or invalid. Entries are eligible for only one SuperLotto Plus 2nd Chance drawing.

How to Win
 Entry deadlines and details will be posted on the Lottery website. Entry deadlines are subject to change by the Lottery. The Lottery will randomly select winners from the eligible internet entries received for each weekly SuperLotto Plus 2nd Chance Drawing. SuperLotto Plus 2nd Chance winners’ names from each drawing will be posted on the Lottery website. Using the contact information provided to the Lottery by the player at registration, the Lottery will notify each winner by e-mail to login into their account for “important information.” Players must provide their true, full legal name when registering for the 2nd Chance program in order to be eligible for any prizes. Any change in name, whether due to marriage or otherwise, must be reflected on the Lottery’s registration page prior to the drawing in order to be eligible for any prize. Only the person identified by name as the Winner on the Lottery website at the time the Winner’s name is posted, which is supplied by the player during registration, may claim the prize from the Lottery. Persons who register with fictitious names will be ineligible to win any prize. Upon login to the Lottery’s website, the winner will be notified of the winning prize. Winners must submit a Winner Claim Form in accordance with Lottery Regulations and procedures. Players may not win more than one prize in each drawing.

Eligibility and Participation
 Participants must be 18-years of age or older. In addition, participants must meet eligibility requirements at the time of the program drawing. To enter SuperLotto Plus 2nd Chance drawings, participants must register as a player on the Lottery website. When registering, participants must provide a valid California address and enter submissions from a location within California to be eligible for SuperLotto Plus 2nd Chance drawings. In addition, participants must provide true and current information. Failure to provide participant’s full legal name at registration will make participant ineligible to receive prizes from the California Lottery. In SuperLotto Plus 2nd Chance Draws, only the person identified by name as the Winner on the Lottery website at the time the Winner’s name is posted, which is supplied by the player during registration, may claim the prize from the California Lottery. Any change in name, whether due to marriage or otherwise, must be reflected on the Lottery’s registration page prior to the drawing in order to be eligible for any prize. Persons who register with fictitious names will be ineligible to win any prize. SuperLotto Plus 2nd Chance is not open to certain Lottery vendors or employees of the California Lottery, their agents, and their family members residing in the same household.

Odds
 The odds of winning any prize vary based on the total number of entries that are received and the total number of entries each player submits. Players may not win more than one prize in each drawing.

Additional Terms
Participants are responsible for complying with these Official Rules in addition to state and federal law and California Lottery Regulations. Proof of age, eligibility, and identity must be furnished upon request. Participants agree to the Lottery’s use of their name, photo, likeness, and hometown for advertising and publicity purposes, without compensation. All taxes are the responsibility of the winner. By participating in the program, and/or accepting any prize or promotional award, participants agree to and do release and hold harmless the California Lottery and its commissioners, employees, agents, and officers from any claim, demand, judgment, award, and/or any liability of any kind whatsoever. The Lottery reserves the right to modify or terminate this program at any time. In the event of a conflict, these Official Rules supersede any applicable general rules or advertisements of the Lottery.

Information Collection and Terms of Service
 It is the Lottery's policy to limit the collection of personal information and protect the personal information we collect and maintain. Learn more about information collection, use and access, read our Privacy Policy and current Terms of Service.

SuperLotto Plus® 2nd Chance Multiplier FAQs

Q: What is the SuperLotto Plus 2nd Chance Multiplier Promotion?
A: The SuperLotto Plus 2nd Chance Multiplier Promotion offers SuperLotto Plus players more entries into the weekly SuperLotto Plus 2nd Chance pool draws with its “multiplier” feature.

Q: When does the SuperLotto Plus 2nd Chance Multiplier Promotion begin?
A: The SuperLotto Plus 2nd Chance Multiplier Promotion begins May 31, 2015.

Q: When does the SuperLotto Plus 2nd Chance Multiplier Promotion end?
A: The SuperLotto Plus 2nd Chance Multiplier Promotion ends July 11, 2015 at 11:59 pm Pacific Time.

Q: How does the “multiplier” feature work?
A: During the promotional period, for certain SuperLotto Plus 2nd Chance webcodes submitted, a player will receive a multiplier that will either give the player 2x or 3x the standard entries into the weekly SuperLotto Plus 2nd Chance pool draws. See details below about the multiplier:

• For every $1.00 – $4.00 SuperLotto Plus purchased per ticket, a player will continue to receive the standard entries into the 2nd Chance system
• For every $5.00 – $9.00 SuperLotto Plus purchased per ticket, a player will receive 2x the standard entries into the 2nd Chance system
• For every $10.00 SuperLotto Plus purchased per ticket, a player will receive 3x the standard entries into the 2nd Chance system

Advance Plays®:
• For every $1.00 – $4.00 SuperLotto Plus Advance Play purchased per ticket, a player will continue to receive the standard entries into the 2nd Chance system
• For every $5.00 – $9.00 SuperLotto Plus Advance Play purchased per ticket, a player will receive 2x the standard entries into the 2nd Chance system
• For every $10.00 + SuperLotto Plus Advance Play purchased per ticket, a player will receive 3x the standard entries into the 2nd Chance system

Q: How does the “multiplier” feature affect the 2nd Chance Code Submission Policy?
A: All 2nd Chance code submissions will be limited to no more than a combined 500 code submissions per calendar per participant. The extra 2nd Chance entries from the multiplier do not affect the 2nd Chance Code Submission Policy. However, the 2nd Chance webcodes does count towards the 500 code submissions per calendar month per participant. For example, if player purchased a $5.00 SuperLotto Plus ticket, the player would receive one (1) 2nd Chance webcode at the bottom of the SuperLotto Plus ticket that will give the player 10 entries into the 2nd Chance system once submitted during the promotional period. The 2nd Chance webcode that was submitted will count as one (1) submission towards the player’s submission count per calendar. The 2nd Chance Code Submission Policy is based on code submissions NOT entries received from special promotions such as the SuperLotto Plus 2nd Chance Multiplier Promotion.

A: Five (5) winners of $15,000 each. Total of $75,000 in prizes awarded every week!

Q: How does a player enter into the weekly SuperLotto Plus 2nd Chance draws?
A: A player must sign into his/her 2nd Chance account. From there, a player can submit his/her SuperLotto Plus 2nd Chance webcodes on the SuperLotto Plus 2nd Chance page.

Q: How does a player register for a 2nd Chance account?
A: To register for a 2nd Chance account, sign up here.

Q: When are the weekly SuperLotto Plus 2nd Chance draws?
A: Draws are conducted within three business days of the entry deadline. Entry deadline is 11:59 pm every Saturday.
